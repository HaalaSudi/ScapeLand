Welcome to ScapeLand, the place you want to be for all things landscape. Each month we are proud to present a landscape architect, a landscape artist and a landscape photographer for landscape enthusiasts. We try to provide both knowledge and inspiration to upcoming artists and entertainment to those that just love landscapes. In this monthly issue we will be introducing to you three artists in their own right.

Lancelot "Capability" Brown

The first being a landscape architect of the name Lancelot Brown or 'Capability Brown' as he is more widely recognised. This landscape architect born in the 1700s is one of the household names to landscape enthusiasts. We shall be delving into his history and his impressive portfolio of work.

Stephen Brown

The second person we shall be presenting to you is an artists named Stephen Brown. While not well known, this british based artist brings a type of serenity and calmness to his canvas. We will be discussing the art of Stephen Brown as well as his preperations, tools and muses.

Franco Fontana

Last but not least is one of the most unique landscape photographers of his time, Franco Fontana. This Italian photographer knows how to bring flare and bold colours to his photos while keeping the realistic element in the landscape he is portraying. It will be interesting to explore more of his work as well as learn more about his inspirations and motivations.
